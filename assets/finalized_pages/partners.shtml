How We Build

Transform for Equity is a consultancy that helps organizations achieve racial equity. They do this by providing customized facilitation and coaching support for systemic racial equity transformation. They have over 25 years of experience working with organizations in the public and private sector across the country. Their work is grounded in the belief that racial equity is essential for a just and thriving society.

Strategic Collaboration

Promote courageous spaces to increase team connection and solve organizational challenges.

Workshops

Tailor and target workshops to increase connection, knowledge, and spur individual and institutional change.

Organizational Assessment

Determine where structural inequities exist and provide actionable and realistic strategies to improve diversity, equity, and belonging within the institution.

Coaching

Empower leaders to advance DEIB strategies throughout the organization.

Who We Support

Non Profits

In addition to enhancing the mission and values of nonprofit organizations, employing DEIB practices also increases job performance and innovation.

Religious Organizations

Spaces of belonging are a core value to many religious institutions where supporting a diverse membership is a moral and growth imperative.

Corporations

Workplace diversity correlates with increased profits, better problem solving and greater creativity.

Schools

Preschool and school age children deserve and desire communities of equity and belonging.
